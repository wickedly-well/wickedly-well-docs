## Export Compliance

### Does your app use encryption?
**Yes** - The app uses standard HTTPS/TLS for network communication.

### Does your app qualify for any exemptions?
**Yes** - The app only uses standard encryption provided by the operating system (HTTPS via URLSession). It qualifies for the encryption exemption under Category 5, Part 2 of the EAR.

### Set the export compliance flag in Info.plist:
```xml
<key>ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption</key>
<false/>
```
